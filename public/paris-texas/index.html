Gracefully slow-moving, much like the flow of the course of life. What Wim Wenders creates and presents in PARIS, TEXAS is one of the most beautifully moving tales I've come across in my entire life. Yet reviewing this film was a struggle - for I get different perspectives each time I visit this great picture, but one constant thing is the satisfaction I get from every watch.

Paris, Texas only grows to captivate me more upon each revisit.
